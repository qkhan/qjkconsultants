Commission Processing System

(Finsure Finance and Insurance)

For any multiple brokers dealing organization or broker aggregator, commission processing is a burdensome and hard task to handle. Gone are the days when brokerage commissions were to be divided between a handful of brokers. Nowadays, companies manage easily up to hundreds of brokers each with a unique profile and commission rates. Companies had difficulties keeping the track and disbursing payment to brokers efficiently and quickly until when technological and automated commission processing system came to their rescue.


Project Overview


  1. The automated transaction processing system comes with powerful features and tools.

  2. The state of art commission processing system is developed in such a way it enables the brokerage company to enter, store, analyse and automate commissions payment to their brokers. The rich set featured and high configuration makes it ideal for any brokerage model and organization don’t have to spend money to redesign and readjust the existing commission processing procedure to adapt to the new system.

  3. Companies can easily distribute commissions between brokers in a matter of minutes compared to when manually done taking many days and even weeks.

  4. The rich set featured and high configuration makes it ideal for any brokerage model and organization don’t have to spend money to redesign and readjust the existing commission processing procedure to adapt to the new system.

Developed Solution


Commission processing system is a developed and designed standalone software system that implements:

  1. Complex recipient hierarchies and fully configurable parameter-driven calculation algorithms.

  2. Import and match received commission data, validate against a suite of data quality rules, reconcile against bank accounts and manage suspense transaction.

  3. Automated invoice generation and transmission and recipient payment.

  4. Integrate commission processing with financial, business intelligence (BI), Data warehouse (DW), CRM and lending systems.

  5. Communicate commission data electronically with suppliers and customers.

  6. Automating transaction data reconciliation and payout processing
  7. Developing streamlined brokerage compensation management solution

Commission Processing Screen


The Commission Processing Screen may be used to perform the following activities:

  1. Select commission detail records for payment.
  2. Review and manually edit the commission details.
  3. Place commission details records on hold.
  4. Search for and attach reservations to the agent for commission payment.
  5. Detach commission detail records from an agent or source.
  6. Pay commissions on selected commission details.
  7. Create a Transfer Report.
  8. Print a Commissions Payable report for the highlighted agent, or source in the top frame. This report shows held payments as well as AR payments.
  9. Place the selected guest's commission payment on hold to offset the agent's or source's accounts receivable account.
  10. Prepare files for EFT.

Key features


The state of the art commission management has the following features and capabilities:

  1. The commission processing system has been developed with the following technologies: JavaScript, Mod PERL, Ajax, JASON and Object Oriented PERL 5.

  2. The system uses a highly efficient adaptable and resource processing and calculation algorithms

  3. High level of data integrity is maintained by the deployment of multiple data validation and cleansing method which ensure the data remains incorrect form and format.

  4. The system offers a full support reconfiguration according to channel networks and business models.

  5. Full menu-driven, profile-based and end-user security settings provide a clear separation of duties, giving the administrator control, confidence, and accountability.
  6. Detailed report generation that includes agent commissions report, RCTIs and commission executive summaries in PDF, EXCEL, and WORD format.
  7. Full support of commission processing through bureau processing and additional administrative staff or a full outsource model.
  8. Full automation capability of transaction processing, commission holding, and distribution through local banking and automatic ABA file generation.
  9. Place the selected guest's commission payment on hold to offset the agent's or source's accounts receivable account.
  10. Prepare files for EFT.
