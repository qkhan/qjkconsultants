Product/Market Segmentation Reporting Tool



Finsure Group is one of the fastest growing aggregation business in the industry. Through its support and partnership with independent brands, the group has achieved that goal by continuing to offer the strongest value proposition in the market to mortgage brokers and financial planners help customers achieve their financial goals.


Project Overview


  1. Market Segmentation is an integral part of a company's marketing strategy. It is the process of breaking down a larger target market into smaller, more homogeneous groups of customers that the company can more efficiently market to.

  2. Product segmentation has assumed an important role as products and brands continue to increase quickly.

  3. Product segmentation enables an organization to better satisfy the needs of its potential customers. The objective of market segmentation is realization of maximum gains by better utilization of segments.

  4. For small businesses this might be a simple process but for multinational companies this process involves huge amounts of data including customer interviews, product reviews and surveys etc.

  5. To automate the process of segmentation report generation, a product and marketing segmentation reporting tool was developed for Finsure Australia.

Project Features and Outcomes


  1. The developed project uses data from various sources and generates detailed market segmentation reports.

  2. The tool helps the management team better understand the needs and taste of customer differ from customer to customer because of age and other reasons. Creation of separate offers for each and every segment/class provides customers with a better solution

  3. Product segmentation reports has helped the sales team to accelerate sales. Customers are encouraged to "trade-up" after being introduced to a particular product with an introductory, lower-priced product.

  4. By marketing products that appeal to customers at different life stages, the company can retain customers who might otherwise switch to competing products and brands by satisfying their needs.

  5. The disposable incomes of customers are different therefore customers react differently to price, that’s why product segmentation reporting has enabled the marketing team to increase the average price and subsequently boost their profits.

  6. The market and product segmentation tool has helped place the company’s research on a firm factual foundation. It has uncovered the characteristics of the market that were not visible before and has identified ways of dividing and approaching the market that will be particularly effective. If these ways are not evident to competitors, the marketing impact of segmentation research can be even more beneficial.
