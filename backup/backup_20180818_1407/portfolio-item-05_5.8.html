Definition


Developed an Income protection calculator for the Super Fund “AMIST”, which determined the annual, monthly fortnightly and weekly benefit based on the income of the employer.

Occupation Status / Worker Type Description:

  1. The user selects the occupation status:
    1. if the status is Manual Worker, the following text explains the worker Type:
      1. A manual worker is a member who is employed to perform manual and or physical duties in an abattoir, processing plant, butcher's shop or similar environment.

    2. If the status is office worker, then the text detailing the type of work is:
      1. An office worker is a member who performs only non-manual duties and works at least 75% of the time in an office environment and has done so for the last 12 months. Office workers pay a lower premium because they are less likely to be injured while working.




Gross Annual Salary greater than 800,000

  1. The user puts in Gross Annual Salary. If the gross annual salary is greater than 800,000, the message appears: As per the policy, your salary exceeds the maximum benefit limit of $50,000 per month. For further Information, please contact AMIST on 1800 808 614.


Gross Annual Salary greater than 400,000 and less than 800,000

  1. If the gross annual salary is greater than 400,000 and less than 800,000, the message appears: As per the policy, your salary exceeds the Automatic Acceptance Limit (AAL) of $25,000 per month, therefore in order to have cover above the Automatic Acceptance Limit you are required to contact AMIST on 1800 808 614.


Income Protection Calculation for the manual worker

  1. Let’s say the Gross Annual Salary is $70,000 for the manual worker
    THEN
    The Annual Benefit is calculated as:
    For
    1. Annual Benefit = Gross Annual Salary * 0.75
      Annual Benefit = 70,000 * 0.75 = 52500
    2. Monthly Benefit = Annual Benefit / 12
      Monthly Benefit = 52500 / 12 = 4375
    3. Fortnightly Benefit = Annual Benefit / 26;
      Fortnightly Benefit = 52500 / 26 = 2019.23
    4. Weekly Benefit = Annual Benefit / 26;
      Weekly Benefit = 52500 / 52 = 1009.62

  2. Number of Units required for full Protection:
    If Gross Salary < 32000 THEN
    Number of Units Required = 4;

  3. ELSE IF Gross Salary is greater than or equal to 32000 THEN
    Number of Units Required = Annual Benefit / 6000;
    Number of Units Required = 52500 / 6000 = 8.75 = 9 (Rounded Up)


  4. IF the Occupation Type is “Manual Worker”
    THEN
    Annual total Insurance Cost = Number of Units * 39
    Annual total Insurance Cost = 9 * 39 = 351
    Monthly total Insurance Cost = Number of Units * 39 / 12
    Monthly total Insurance Cost = 9 * 39 / 12 = 29.5


  5. Fortnightly total Insurance Cost = Number of Units*39/26=13.5
    Fortnightly total Insurance Cost = 9 * 39 / 26 = 13.5
    Weekly total Insurance Cost = Number of Units * 39 / 52
    Weekly total Insurance Cost = 9 * 39 / 52 = 6.75
