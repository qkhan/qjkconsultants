Triangulaton Analysis Reporting System

(Beazley Group)

Project Overview


  1. Triangulation or Net loss analysis is an important part of actuarial analysis in any insurance business. Actuarial analysis is an essential task performed by insurance companies to analyze data and estimate the probability of an insurance claim being filed for a given event.

  2. This work allows insurance companies to predict with a reasonable degree of accuracy the amount of claims they will pay out, which helps them determine what premiums they must charge to remain profitable.

  3. Acceptably accurate predictions require a lot of qualitative and quantitative data and it is extremely tedious and time consuming for underwriters to perform such predictive modeling manually.

  4. To make life easier for the analysts, an automated Triangulation/Net Loss analysis and reporting system was developed for underwriting team of Beazley UK, which is a specialist insurer with three decades of experience in providing clients with the highest standards of underwriting and claims service worldwide.

  5. Beazely is serving customers in 8 countries across Europe and America.

Developed Solution/Project Features


  1. The Triangulation (Net Loss Ratio) Analysis and reporting system is designed by keeping automation, accuracy and efficiency as key objective. It is designed for the Underwriting team of the company, enabling them to examine existing claims & premium data quickly to strengthen interpretations and improve policy and programs.

  2. The developed system, combines information from quantitative and qualitative analysis, and shows the percent of health plan premiums that were spent on claims costs for individual and small employer health plan markets for insurance companies.


  3. Underwriters can analyze the triangulation reports per Insurance facility or per individual clients. The list of Binder Facilities is organized per year and clients under each Binder year are grouped and listed out by expanding the Binder Year.


  4. The underwriters can also generate “Individual Client Triangulation Reports”, which gives the details of all the clients and the policies attached with each client. The user can click on a policy and a popup windows appears, showing the Loss Ratio calculation for the policy.


  5. The user can also select multiple clients and generate Loss ratio report for multiple clients, both combined and Individual clients.
